## Statements
She Said: [Text] -> Outputs [Text]
She Remembered: [Variable] = [Value] -> Makes [Variable] Remembers [Value]
She Left -> Ends Code.
She Checked If: {Condition} -> Anything inside will be executed IF statement is true
She Checked While: {Condition} -> Anything inside will be executed WHILE statement is true
